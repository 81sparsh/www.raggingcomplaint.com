About Us

Social evils exist in different types. Emotions, Love, Traditions, Culture may or may not stand the test of time and travel across borders, but social evils are an exception to the fact. One of such ‘Social Evils’ is what we call “Ragging”.

Ragging is basically bullying, harassment, and disorderly conduct acted upon the ‘Freshers’ or the lower classmen in a school or college by the Seniors of those Institutions. A recent incident at a prestigious school has shown that this evil has not yet come to an end.

A Practice popularized from the British era continuing till this day and age. It is one of the many evil heirlooms practiced by the British to teach social hierarchy and compel the students to be more “disciplined”. This evil practice has only grown worse in its form and type. In present-day and age, several institutions have faced the wrath of the public as more and more cases of Ragging surfaced.

A recent survey highlighted the fact that most cases of these malpractices happen with the purpose to get the ones new into the institution familiar with the traditions and that they remain disciplined and respect the Students from the Senior Sections.

In a recent case, an Ugly Ragging Incident caused the victim to commit suicide. The tragedy took place at one of the most Prestigious Schools in the City. The Ugly episode began when the victim, a male student from the 11th Standard was asked to slap a Girl from the Same Class. Upon Refusing to commit this heinous and horrendous task, the group from the 12th Standard started harassing the victim Mentally to an extent where death seemed more trouble-free to Him. Upon Investigation by the police, it was found that the mastermind behind the harassment was the ‘Prefect’ from the 12th Standard.

A Prefect is the one considered to be a teacher’s Substitute. The grave misuse of authority by the prefect is nothing but a sin and unacceptable. Such Misconducts occur in most institutions in the country with almost 100 students every year, succumbing to mental harassment and sometimes physical.

An educational institution is a place where parents send their children to educate themselves into a better human being and gain knowledge and hence to maintain the sanctity of education and values, such Evil Practices must be uprooted as soon as possible.

Ragging, in its various forms, not only tarnishes the educational experience but also leaves lasting scars on the victims' psyche. The repercussions extend beyond the immediate individuals involved, impacting families, communities, and society at large.

Efforts to eradicate ragging should extend beyond punitive measures. Educational institutions should focus on creating a culture of inclusion and mentorship, where seniors guide juniors in a positive manner, fostering a sense of camaraderie rather than fear.

Public awareness campaigns can play a crucial role in addressing this issue. By highlighting the damaging effects of ragging and promoting a culture of empathy, society can collectively work towards eliminating this menace from the roots.

The psychological toll on victims often goes unnoticed. Educational institutions should invest in counseling services, creating a support system for those who have experienced ragging. Timely intervention can prevent long-term emotional trauma.

The international community should also collaborate to share best practices and strategies for tackling ragging. By learning from successful interventions globally, countries can strengthen their resolve to create safe learning environments for all students.

Legislative measures should not only focus on punitive actions but also on fostering a sense of responsibility among students. Inculcating values of mutual respect and empathy can contribute to a sustainable solution to the problem of ragging.

Parents play a pivotal role in shaping their children's attitudes. It is essential for parents to instill values of kindness and respect, encouraging open communication about their children's experiences in educational institutions.

The media can act as a powerful ally in the fight against ragging. Responsible reporting can shed light on the prevalence of this issue and hold institutions accountable for maintaining a safe learning environment.

Technology can be harnessed to create anonymous reporting systems, allowing students to report incidents without fear of reprisal. This empowers individuals to speak out against ragging and facilitates swift action against perpetrators.

Educational institutions should conduct regular workshops and training sessions for both students and faculty to raise awareness about the detrimental effects of ragging and promote a culture of mutual respect.

Alumni networks can play a crucial role in shaping institutional culture. Alumni who have experienced ragging can share their stories and advocate for change, inspiring current students to stand against this harmful practice.

Research studies should be encouraged to delve deeper into the root causes of ragging and its impact on individuals and society. Evidence-based interventions can be developed to address the specific challenges posed by this social issue.

Creating safe spaces within educational institutions, where students feel comfortable reporting incidents of ragging, is essential. A culture of zero tolerance should be actively promoted, ensuring that every member of the institution is aware of the consequences of engaging in ragging.

Peer support programs can be established to encourage positive interactions between seniors and juniors. Pairing incoming students with mentors who exemplify values of respect and empathy can significantly reduce the likelihood of ragging incidents.

International bodies, such as UNESCO, should advocate for the eradication of ragging on a global scale. Collaborative efforts can lead to the development of comprehensive strategies that address the nuances of this issue in diverse cultural and educational contexts.

Empowering student leaders to take a stand against ragging is crucial. Student-led initiatives and campaigns can create a groundswell of opposition, making it clear that the majority of students reject and condemn such harmful practices.

Creating avenues for open dialogue between students, faculty, and administrators can foster a sense of community within educational institutions. Transparent communication channels enable swift resolution of conflicts and contribute to the prevention of ragging incidents.

Investing in proactive measures, such as team-building activities and orientation programs, can set a positive tone for the academic year. These initiatives help build a sense of belonging and discourage the adoption of harmful practices like ragging.

Ragging is a societal problem that requires a multifaceted approach. Collaboration between educational institutions, government bodies, non-profit organizations, and the wider community is essential to create lasting change.

In conclusion, the fight against ragging demands concerted efforts at various levels. By addressing the root causes, fostering a culture of respect, and implementing proactive measures, we can work towards creating educational environments that nurture growth, learning, and mutual understanding.

The Government and the Concerned authorities should pass a strict law complimenting the mass abolishment of this cruelty called “Ragging”. A strict Punishment will force many perpetrators to think before committing such atrocious acts in the future. Every institution should also keep a close eye into making sure they prevent such crimes to take place.
